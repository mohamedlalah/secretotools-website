FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SecretoTools TV?

It is a professional diagnostic toolkit for Android TV and Google TV devices.

Does the app collect personal data?

No. The app is designed to display technical information locally on the device and does not sell or upload personal information to our servers.

Why can the app only show selected installed apps?

Android privacy rules limit app discovery. SecretoTools TV only checks supported apps explicitly declared by the developer.

Is SecretoTools TV for legal use only?

Yes. It is a diagnostic and information utility and must be used only for lawful purposes.
