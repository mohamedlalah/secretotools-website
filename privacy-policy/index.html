Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SecretoTools TV handles information. SecretoTools TV is an Android TV diagnostic toolkit developed by SecretoTools / Mohamed LALAH.

Information we collect

SecretoTools TV does not collect, sell, share, upload, or transmit personal information to our servers.

The app may display technical information locally on your Android TV device, such as device status, storage, network state, codec capabilities, and selected supported installed applications. This information is used only inside the app and remains on your device.

Installed applications visibility

For privacy reasons, Android limits application discovery. SecretoTools TV only detects applications explicitly declared as supported by the developer. Applications outside this supported list cannot be displayed.

Permissions

  • Network state: used to show network connection status.
  • Wi-Fi state: used to show Wi-Fi related device information when available.

The app does not request camera, microphone, contacts, SMS, precise location, or external storage permissions.

Third parties

SecretoTools TV does not include third-party advertising SDKs and does not share user data with advertisers.

Children

SecretoTools TV is a utility app and is not directed to children. It does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes. The updated version will be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at support@secretotools.com.
